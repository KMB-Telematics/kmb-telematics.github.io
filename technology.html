A New Generation of High Resolution Imaging Radars

KMB Telematics makes cutting-edge imaging radar systems for autonomous machines. By merging advanced, low-cost radar chips with proprietary signal processing techniques, we have transformed radar into a software-defined solution. Our intelligent radar systems deliver high fidelity information about the surrounding environment, providing high-quality data for enhanced autonomy in various settings, including ADAS, on land and in the air, and in all types of weather conditions. KMB radar technology can produce high-resolution images. See for example this radar image of a bridge.



Fully Customizable Imaging Technology

Our Software-defined architecture can be customized to meet customer needs, including:

  • Higher resolution
  • Custom field of view
  • Longer/shorter range
  • Faster update rate
  • Choice of operating frequency: 24 GHz, 60 GHz, 77 GHz
  • Higher dynamic range



Revolutionary Zero Doppler™ Technology

Our proprietary Zero Doppler™ technique enables us to image moving as well as stationary targets even when our radar system is on the move.
