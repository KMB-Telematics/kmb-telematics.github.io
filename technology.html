A New Generation of High Resolution Imaging Radars

KMB Telematics makes cutting-edge imaging radar systems for autonomous machines. By combining low-cost COTS RF chips with proprietary signal processing techniques, we turn radar into a software problem. Our intelligent radar systems deliver high fidelity information about the surrounding environment, providing high-quality data for enhanced autonomy in all weather, day or night. The technology scales to very high resolution. See for example this radar image of a bridge.



Revolutionary Zero Doppler™ Technology

Our patented Zero Doppler™ technique enables our radar to image moving and stationary targets in the same scene without the need for accurate position/navigation/timing (PNT) information for unprecedented robustness. This means the system can be used on the move, or in areas where accurate PNT information is unavailable.



Fully Customizable Architecture

The software-defined architecture is very flexible. Many parameters can be customized to meet customer needs, including:

  • Higher resolution
  • Higher dynamic range
  • Higher sensitivity
  • Longer/shorter range
  • Faster update rate
  • Choice of operating frequency: 15 GHz, 24 GHz, 60 GHz, 77 GHz
  • Lower power consumption
